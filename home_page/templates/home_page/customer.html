ABOUT US


The company was initially formed by two young entrepreneurs from Tamilnadu. Vishnubalaji and Raghul, the founders of the company, who completed their graduation from PSG College of Technology, Coimbatore. The name 'VRhere.com' was derived from there initial letters of their names (V)ishnubalaji, (R)aghul, that would be pronounced as "we are here".


VRhere, is an Indian ridesharing company (TNC) offering services that include peer-to-peer ridesharing, ride service hailing, taxi. The company is based in Coimbatore, Tamilnadu, India. A variety of venture capitalists including Softbank have large stakes in the company.
